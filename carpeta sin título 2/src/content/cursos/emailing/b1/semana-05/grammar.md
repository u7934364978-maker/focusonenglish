# Grammar (B1) · Semana 05

> Objetivo: escribir recaps (resumen) después de reuniones: decisiones claras + action items + responsables + fechas.

## 1) Recap / Summary (frases base)

| Intención | Patrón (EN) | Ejemplo (EN) |
|---|---|---|
| Resumen neutro | Here’s a quick recap of… | Here’s a quick recap of today’s call. |
| Confirmar puntos | Just to recap, … | Just to recap, we agreed on the timeline. |
| Confirmar decisiones | We agreed to + VERB | We agreed to finalize the draft by Friday. |

> Tip: “Here’s a quick recap” suena neutral, no “teacher”.

---

## 2) Action items (quién hace qué)

| Intención | Patrón (EN) | Ejemplo (EN) |
|---|---|---|
| Acción con responsable | [Name] will + VERB | Maria will share the final numbers. |
| Acción propia | I will + VERB | I’ll update the draft today. |
| Deadline suave | by + day/date (if possible) | Maria will share it by Thursday. |

**✅ Correcto**
- Maria will share the numbers by Thursday.
- I’ll send the calendar invite today.

**❌ Incorrecto**
- Maria must share the numbers. (demasiado duro)
- Share the numbers now. (imperativo)

---

## 3) Next steps (cerrar el hilo)

- Next step: I’ll update the draft and share v3.
- If anything is missing, please let me know.
- If that works, I’ll proceed.

> Regla rápida: recap (qué) → action items (quién) → next step (qué pasa después).
