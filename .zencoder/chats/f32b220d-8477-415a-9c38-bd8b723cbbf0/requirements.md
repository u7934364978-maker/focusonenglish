# PRD: 7-Day A1 English Demo Course

## Overview
A 7-day introductory English course for beginners (Level A1) focused on real-life situations. Each day provides a micro-lesson with 6 interactive activities.

## Target Audience
- Absolute beginners (A1 level).
- Native Spanish speakers (content and instructions in Spanish where helpful).

## Course Structure (7 Days)
1.  **Day 1: Greetings & Introductions** (Hello, what's your name?)
2.  **Day 2: Personal Information & Numbers** (Age, phone numbers, I am...)
3.  **Day 3: Family & Relationships** (My family, who is this?)
4.  **Day 4: Daily Routine & Time** (I get up at..., what time is it?)
5.  **Day 5: Food & Ordering** (At the restaurant, I'd like...)
6.  **Day 6: Places in Town & Directions** (Where is the bank?)
7.  **Day 7: Hobbies & Abilities** (I can play football, I like...)

## Content per Day (Lesson)
Each lesson MUST include:
1.  **6 Interactive Activities**:
    -   **Flashcard**: Vocabulary learning with front/back cards.
    -   **Multiple Choice**: Grammar or comprehension questions.
    -   **Drag & Drop**: Sentence building or matching.
    -   **Listening**: Comprehension based on an audio script.
    -   **Speaking**: Voice recording practice for natural phrases.
    -   **Writing**: Simple sentence completion or short response.
2.  **Vocabulary & Key Phrases**: A curated list for the daily topic.
3.  **Audio Assets**: 
    -   Listening script (for the activity).
    -   2 mini-dialogues for situational practice.
4.  **Solutions**: Correct answers for all exercises.

## Technical Requirements
-   **Nivel A1**: Short sentences, simple vocabulary.
-   **Visual Prioritization**: Each flashcard/activity should have a suggested image description.
-   **Language**: English for learning, Spanish for instructions and explanations.
-   **Format**: Compatible with `Lesson` and `Exercise` types in the existing codebase.

## Success Criteria
-   7 complete lessons generated.
-   All activity types implemented per lesson.
-   Content is pedagogically sound for A1 level.
