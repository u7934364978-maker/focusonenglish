# Proposed Cambridge B1 Preliminary (PET) Syllabus Structure (30 Units)

## Module 1: Personal Identification and Daily Life
- **Unit 1**: All About Me (Present Simple, Greetings, Paper 1 Part 1)
- **Unit 2**: Daily Routines & Lifestyles (Adverbs of Frequency, Paper 1 Part 1)
- **Unit 3**: Family and Friends (Possessives, Describing People, Paper 4 Part 1)
- **Unit 4**: My Home and Neighborhood (Prepositions of Place, There is/are, Paper 1 Part 2)
- **Unit 5**: Free Time and Hobbies (Present Continuous vs Simple, Paper 3 Part 1)
- **Unit 6**: Module 1 Review & Exam Practice (Reading Parts 1-2, Speaking Part 1)

## Module 2: The Past and Experiences
- **Unit 7**: Memorable Events (Past Simple: Regular/Irregular, Paper 2 Part 2: Stories)
- **Unit 8**: Childhood Memories (Used to, Past Continuous, Paper 2 Part 2: Stories)
- **Unit 9**: Travel Experiences (Present Perfect for experience, Paper 3 Part 2)
- **Unit 10**: Health and Fitness (Past Simple vs Present Perfect, Paper 1 Part 3)
- **Unit 11**: Shopping and Services (Comparatives & Superlatives, Paper 4 Part 2)
- **Unit 12**: Module 2 Review & Exam Practice (Reading Part 3, Writing Part 2)

## Module 3: Modern World and Communication
- **Unit 13**: Digital Life & Social Media (Future with Will/Going to, Paper 1 Part 4)
- **Unit 14**: Entertainment and Cinema (Relative Clauses, Paper 3 Part 3)
- **Unit 15**: Writing Emails (Informal Tone, Linkers, Paper 2 Part 1)
- **Unit 16**: Food and Eating Out (Quantifiers, Modal verbs for advice, Paper 4 Part 3)
- **Unit 17**: Education and Learning (Passive Voice intro, Paper 1 Part 5)
- **Unit 18**: Module 3 Review & Exam Practice (Listening Part 3, Reading Part 4)

## Module 4: Environment and Future Challenges
- **Unit 19**: Our Planet & Climate Change (Zero & First Conditionals, Paper 1 Part 6)
- **Unit 20**: The Natural World (Modal verbs of possibility, Paper 3 Part 4)
- **Unit 21**: Work and Careers (Second Conditional, Paper 1 Part 5)
- **Unit 22**: Science and Innovation (Reported Speech intro, Paper 4 Part 4)
- **Unit 23**: Culture and Customs (Passive Voice, Paper 3 Part 4)
- **Unit 24**: Module 4 Review & Exam Practice (Reading Parts 5-6, Listening Part 4)

## Module 5: Final Preparation and Mock Exams
- **Unit 25**: Mastering Reading (Focus on Parts 4, 5, 6)
- **Unit 26**: Advanced Writing (Article vs Story techniques)
- **Unit 27**: Listening Strategies (Handling complex dialogues)
- **Unit 28**: Speaking Fluency (Interactive Communication focus)
- **Unit 29**: Full Mock Exam - Paper 1 & 2
- **Unit 30**: Full Mock Exam - Paper 3 & 4
