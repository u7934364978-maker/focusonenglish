# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - English B1 Course (Expanded)

## Feature Overview
Creation of a full 30-unit English B1 (Intermediate) course following Cambridge B1 Preliminary standards.

## Curriculum Design (30 Units)
1.  **Lifestyles & Routines** (*Used to*, Past Simple)
2.  **The World of Work** (Modals of obligation/advice)
3.  **Future & Technology** (*Will*, *Going to*, Pres. Cont.)
4.  **Travel & Experiences** (Past Continuous, Past Perfect)
5.  **Social Issues & Opinions** (1st and 2nd Conditionals)
6.  **Entertainment & Media** (Passive Voice, Relative Clauses)
7.  **Healthy Living** (Present Perfect Continuous, Health vocab)
8.  **Shopping & Money** (Quantifiers, Money/Retail vocab)
9.  **Education & Learning** (Reported Speech statements, Education vocab)
10. **Nature & Wildlife** (Comparisons, Animals/Environment vocab)
11. **Food & Dining Out** (Passive Voice review, Cooking/Restaurant vocab)
12. **Relationships & Feelings** (Relative Clauses review, Emotions/Family vocab)
13. **Crime & Punishment** (Past Perfect review, Justice/Law vocab)
14. **Sport & Leisure** (Modals of ability/possibility, Sport/Hobby vocab)
15. **Music & Festivals** (Adjective order, Festivals/Art vocab)
16. **Weather & Climate Change** (Zero Conditional, Weather vocab)
17. **Business & Entrepreneurship** (Wish/If only, Business/Success vocab)
18. **Inventions & Innovation** (Noun phrases, Science vocab)
19. **History & Heritage** (Used to/Would review, History vocab)
20. **Media & Fake News** (Reporting verbs, Media bias vocab)
21. **City Life vs. Country Life** (Review of contrasts, Advanced urban/rural vocab)
22. **Mysteries & The Unexplained** (Modals of deduction, Mystery vocab)
23. **Personality & Behavior** (Phrasal verbs, Adjectives of character)
24. **Space Exploration** (Future Continuous, Astronomy vocab)
25. **Fashion & Trends** (Present Perfect with for/since, Style vocab)
26. **Charity & Volunteering** (Infinitives and Gerunds, Altruism vocab)
27. **The Arts** (Passive voice advanced, Art expression vocab)
28. **Global Economy** (Conditionals review, Money/Trade vocab)
29. **Cultural Differences** (Question tags, Etiquette/Customs vocab)
30. **Course Review & Exam Prep** (Comprehensive review)

## Functional Requirements
- 30 Interactive lessons at `/curso/ingles-b1/[unitId]`.
- Landing page grid showing all 30 units.
