# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Content Completion & Repair

## 1. Overview
The goal is to fill the gaps identified during the audit, ensuring all 60 units of the English A1 course are complete, follow the "Mateo in London" narrative, and adhere to all pedagogical standards.

## 2. Target Objectives
- **Unit 3 Reconstruction**: Create a full set of 60+ exercises for `unit-3.ts` covering the Verb 'to be' (Negatives/Questions) and Emotions.
- **Narrative Integration**: Ensure every unit references Mateo's journey or the London context to maintain engagement and consistency.
- **Translation Compliance**: Apply the `[[English|Spanish]]` format to all English text across all units, prioritizing units 30-60.
- **Structural Standardization**: Ensure all units follow the `Exercise` interface and include necessary fields like `audioUrl`, `transcript`, and `topicName`.

## 3. Unit 3 Specifics
- **Title**: Verb 'to be': Negatives and Questions.
- **Storyline**: Mateo arrives at his hostel, checks in, and talks about how he feels (tired from the trip, happy to be there).
- **Grammar**: Negative forms (am not, isn't, aren't), Yes/No questions, Short answers.
- **Vocabulary**: Emotions and States (Happy, sad, tired, hungry, thirsty).

## 4. Quality Standards
- **Translations**: Mandatory for all English content.
- **Difficulty**: Progressive within the unit.
- **Context**: Every exercise must feel part of the London narrative.
