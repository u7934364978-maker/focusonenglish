# PRD: Week 16 - Supply Chain & Vendor Relations (Expansion)

## Goal
Expand Week 16 of the B2 Administration course from 15 exercises to 45 exercises and from 4 theory sections to 8 theory sections.

## Content Strategy
The expansion will focus on sustainable procurement (ESG), risk management, and digital transformation in the supply chain.

### Block 2: Sustainability & Risk Management
- **Theory 5**: Sustainable Procurement (ESG criteria).
- **Theory 6**: Risk Management in the Supply Chain (Disruptions, contingencies).
- **Exercises 16-30**:
  - Identifying ESG factors in vendor selection.
  - Case studies on supply chain disruptions.
  - Vocabulary: *due diligence*, *contingency plan*, *single sourcing* vs *multi-sourcing*.
  - Grammar: Practice with the subjunctive mood in formal requests.

### Block 3: Strategic Partnerships & E-Procurement
- **Theory 7**: Moving from Vendors to Strategic Partners.
- **Theory 8**: E-procurement and Digital Tools (ERP, automated POs).
- **Exercises 31-45**:
  - Analyzing vendor performance (KPIs).
  - Benefits of digital procurement systems.
  - Listening placeholder for a contract negotiation.
  - Writing tasks for requesting contract renewals or terminations.

## Technical Requirements
- Follow existing JSON schema for `theory.json` and `exercises.json`.
- Maintain exercise ID format: `adm-b2-w16-exXX`.
- Ensure all exercises have a `modelAnswer` or `answerIndex` and an `explanation` (where applicable).
