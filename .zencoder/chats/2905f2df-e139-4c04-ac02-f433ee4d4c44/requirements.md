# PRD - Banca y Finanzas Course Completion (Trimestre 1)

## Overview
The "Banca y Finanzas" (Finance) course currently has content for levels B1, B2, and C1. Levels A1 and A2 are missing their content files (theory, exercises, and exams) for the first trimester.

## Objectives
- Complete the first trimester (Weeks 1-12) for levels A1 and A2.
- Ensure content follows the curriculum defined in `src/lib/sectors/finance-curriculum.ts`.
- Maintain consistency with existing levels (B1, B2, C1) in terms of JSON structure and quality.

## Requirements

### Content Structure
Each week must contain:
1. `theory.json`: Markdown content explaining the week's topics, grammar, and vocabulary.
2. `exercises.json`: A list of multiple-choice exercises related to the theory.
3. `exam.json`: A final assessment for the week.

### Level-Specific Goals (Trimestre 1)

#### Level A1: Números y Operaciones Básicas
- **Week 1-3**: Números grandes, monedas, horarios bancarios.
- **Week 4-6**: Saludos, información personal, formularios simples.
- **Week 7-9**: Cortesía en la oficina, vocabulario básico de oficina.
- **Week 10-12**: Repaso y examen final del trimestre.

#### Level A2: Trámites Bancarios y Pasado
- **Week 1-3**: Apertura de cuentas y requisitos.
- **Week 4-6**: Descripción de transacciones pasadas (Past Simple).
- **Week 7-9**: Problemas comunes (pérdida de tarjetas) y comparaciones.
- **Week 10-12**: Repaso y examen final del trimestre.

## Success Criteria
- Files exist in `src/content/cursos/trabajo/finanzas/[a1/a2]/trimestre1/semana[01-12]/`.
- Content is technically accurate and pedagogically appropriate for the level.
- All JSON files are valid and follow the project schema.
