# A2 Narrative Course - Learning Outcomes by Unit (Cambridge A2 Level)

## Module 1: Personal Growth & Adaptation (Units 1-10)

### Unit 1: Settling Into a New Environment
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Describe where you live and daily household routines
- ✅ Use Present Simple affirmative, negative, and questions
- ✅ Master household vocabulary: rooms, furniture, appliances, chores
- ✅ Understand time expressions with habitual actions
- ✅ Form sentences with correct subject-verb agreement
- ✅ Communicate about simple daily activities

**Grammar:** Present Simple (all persons), affirmative/negative/questions
**Vocabulary:** House, rooms, furniture, household chores, daily routines
**Communicative Function:** Describe where you live and what you do there

---

### Unit 2: Making New Friends
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Talk about hobbies and interests
- ✅ Initiate and sustain simple conversations
- ✅ Use Present Simple for habits and preferences (like/love/enjoy + -ing)
- ✅ Ask about and describe leisure activities
- ✅ Understand social formulas and greetings
- ✅ Express frequency with adverbs (always, usually, sometimes, never)

**Grammar:** Present Simple + gerund (like/enjoy doing), frequency adverbs
**Vocabulary:** Hobbies, leisure activities, sports, entertainment, friends
**Communicative Function:** Talk about what you like to do in your free time

---

### Unit 3: Cultural Differences & Adaptation
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Compare things using comparative adjectives
- ✅ Use Present Perfect to describe life experiences
- ✅ Understand cultural customs and social differences
- ✅ Describe changes and adaptations
- ✅ Recognize and use intensifiers (very, much more, quite)
- ✅ Express novelty and past experiences relevant to present

**Grammar:** Comparatives (regular and irregular), Present Perfect with ever/never/already/just
**Vocabulary:** Cultural customs, differences, adaptation, comparisons
**Communicative Function:** Discuss what is different about a new place and how you've adapted

---

### Unit 4: Food & Dining Culture
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Distinguish between countable and uncountable nouns
- ✅ Use correct quantity expressions (some, any, much, many, a few, a little)
- ✅ Order food politely in a restaurant
- ✅ Describe food preferences and dietary needs
- ✅ Understand cooking methods and food preparation vocabulary
- ✅ Make requests using polite forms

**Grammar:** Countable vs. Uncountable nouns, some/any/much/many, polite requests
**Vocabulary:** Food, meals, cooking methods, restaurants, dietary terms
**Communicative Function:** Order food, describe preferences, understand menus

---

### Unit 5: Weekend Activities & Leisure
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Talk about future plans using "going to"
- ✅ Discuss weekend and free-time activities
- ✅ Ask and answer about plans (near future)
- ✅ Use Present Continuous for arrangements
- ✅ Understand time expressions for future (this weekend, next week, etc.)
- ✅ Make and respond to invitations

**Grammar:** Going to (affirmative, negative, questions), Present Continuous for future plans
**Vocabulary:** Leisure activities, weekend plans, entertainment, sports
**Communicative Function:** Discuss what you're going to do and make plans with others

---

### Unit 6: Social Interactions & Customs
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Use modal verbs for permission: Can, Could, May
- ✅ Understand social etiquette and politeness
- ✅ Ask for and give permission appropriately
- ✅ Recognize formal vs. informal contexts
- ✅ Use polite expressions in social situations
- ✅ Understand degrees of politeness

**Grammar:** Modal verbs (Can, Could, May) for permission and ability
**Vocabulary:** Social customs, politeness, etiquette, formal language
**Communicative Function:** Ask for permission, understand social rules, be polite

---

### Unit 7: Communication & Staying Connected
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Use Past Simple with irregular verbs
- ✅ Narrate past events and experiences
- ✅ Understand communication methods and technology
- ✅ Tell stories about what happened
- ✅ Recognize common irregular verbs in context
- ✅ Use time expressions with past tense (ago, last week, etc.)

**Grammar:** Past Simple (regular and irregular verbs), time expressions
**Vocabulary:** Communication methods, technology, past actions, time references
**Communicative Function:** Tell what happened, describe past experiences

---

### Unit 8: Money & Financial Independence
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Understand and use numbers correctly (especially prices)
- ✅ Talk about money and costs using Past Simple and Present Simple
- ✅ Understand financial vocabulary: price, cost, spend, save, earn
- ✅ Discuss personal finances and budgeting
- ✅ Make and understand transactions
- ✅ Ask about and provide information on prices

**Grammar:** Numbers/prices, Past Simple for financial narratives, Present Simple for habits
**Vocabulary:** Money, prices, financial terms, spending, saving, earning
**Communicative Function:** Ask and answer about prices, discuss money matters

---

### Unit 9: Health & Fitness
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Use modal verbs for obligation and advice: Should, Must, Have to
- ✅ Understand health and fitness vocabulary
- ✅ Give and understand health-related advice
- ✅ Describe symptoms and health issues simply
- ✅ Understand wellness and fitness routines
- ✅ Recognize obligation vs. recommendation

**Grammar:** Modal verbs (Should, Must, Have to, Don't have to), negatives
**Vocabulary:** Health, illness, fitness, exercise, wellness, body parts
**Communicative Function:** Discuss health, give advice, describe fitness routines

---

### Unit 10: Module 1 Review & Assessment
**Cambridge A2 Objectives:**
- ✅ Integrate and apply all Unit 1-9 grammar and vocabulary
- ✅ Understand and produce connected discourse
- ✅ Answer comprehension questions about longer texts
- ✅ Demonstrate mastery of Present Simple, Past Simple, comparatives, modals
- ✅ Use all learned vocabulary in context
- ✅ Show understanding of A2-level functional language

**Grammar:** All Module 1 grammar
**Vocabulary:** All Module 1 vocabulary
**Communicative Function:** Integrated skills - reading, writing, listening (via prompts), speaking simulation

## Cambridge A2 (Elementary) Overall Competencies Covered:

✅ **Can understand sentences and frequently used expressions** related to common themes
✅ **Can communicate in simple, routine tasks** requiring direct exchange of information
✅ **Can describe in simple terms** aspects of their background, environment, and needs
✅ **Can handle everyday social interactions** (greetings, polite requests, small talk)
✅ **Can tell simple stories** and recount familiar experiences
✅ **Can ask and answer simple questions** about present and past
✅ **Can make simple future plans** using going to and will
✅ **Can understand and give basic advice** and express obligation
✅ **Can recognize cultural differences** and adapt appropriately
✅ **Can sustain conversations** on familiar topics (work, family, hobbies, travel)
