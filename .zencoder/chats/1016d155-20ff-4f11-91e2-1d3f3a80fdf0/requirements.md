# Pedagogical Action Plan: Generic English Completion (A1-C2)

## 1. Objectives
- **Complete the CEFR Pyramid**: Fill the missing C1 and C2 levels and replace B2 placeholders.
- **Cambridge Alignment**: Ensure tasks reflect the typology and difficulty of FCE (B2), CAE (C1), and CPE (C2).
- **Skill Diversification**: Shift from purely "receptive/matching" tasks to "productive/structural" tasks.

## 2. Level-Specific Strategy

### Level B2 (Upper Intermediate - First)
- **Current State**: 30 units of placeholder data.
- **Action**: 
    - Replace placeholders with 60 high-quality units.
    - Introduce **Key Word Transformations** (Sentence focus).
    - Introduce **Multiple Choice Cloze** (Vocabulary/Collocation focus).
    - Focus on Phrasal Verbs and Dependent Prepositions.

### Level C1 (Advanced - Advanced)
- **Current State**: 0 units.
- **Action**: 
    - Create 60 units focusing on **Register and Nuance**.
    - Introduce **Word Formation** (Complex suffixes/prefixes).
    - Introduce **Cross-text Multiple Matching** (Reading skills).
    - Focus on Idiomatic expressions and formal discourse markers.

### Level C2 (Proficiency - Proficiency)
- **Current State**: 0 units.
- **Action**: 
    - Create 40 units focusing on **Native-like Mastery**.
    - Introduce **Open Cloze** (Grammar/Structure focus without hints).
    - Focus on subtle connotation, irony, and advanced rhetoric.

## 3. Global Quality Enhancements (A1-B1 Refinement)
- **Audit existing A1-B1**: Ensure instructions are clear and level-appropriate.
- **Audio Enrichment**: Replace robotic TTS with more natural prosody or varied accents where possible.

## 4. Success Metrics
- **B2-C2 Density**: 15+ interactions per unit.
- **Typology Balance**: 30% Grammar, 30% Vocabulary, 40% Skills (Reading/Use of English).
