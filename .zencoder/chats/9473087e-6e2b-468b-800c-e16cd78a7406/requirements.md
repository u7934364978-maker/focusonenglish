# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Course "Negociaciones B2" Excellence & Expansion

## Overview
The goal is to transform the "Ingl√©s para Negociaciones B2" course into a top-tier learning experience by significantly improving the User Experience (UX) and expanding the content to 50 practices per unit.

## Target Audience
Intermediate to Upper-Intermediate (B2) students looking to master English for business negotiations.

## Key Objectives
1. **UX Excellence**: Upgrade the course interface and interactions to provide a seamless, engaging, and highly professional learning experience.
2. **Content Expansion**: Scale each of the 4 units (lessons) in the course to include 50 exercises each, covering various aspects of negotiations (Vocabulary, Grammar, Listening, Speaking, Reading).

## UX Requirements ("Excellence")
### 1. Visual & Interactive Feedback
- **Celebration Animations**: Implement confetti or specialized animations when a student completes a block or a unit.
- **Immediate Feedback**: Enhance the visual distinction between correct and incorrect answers with subtle but clear animations (e.g., green pulse for correct, red shake for incorrect).
- **Sound Effects**: Add optional sound effects for success and failure (with a toggle to mute).

### 2. Progress & Navigation
- **Granular Progress**: Show progress at the question level within a block, not just at the block level.
- **Quick Navigation**: Allow students to jump between questions within a block if they want to review.
- **Session Persistence**: Ensure progress is saved in real-time so users can resume exactly where they left off.

### 3. Design Refinement
- **Focused UI**: Reduce visual noise during exercises to help students concentrate on the content.
- **Professional Aesthetics**: Use a color palette and typography that reflects a business environment (Blue, Slate, White).
- **Responsive Design**: Optimize all exercise types for mobile devices.

### 4. Gamification Integration
- **Real-time XP**: Show XP being earned immediately after each correct answer.
- **Streaks**: Display a "Correct Answer Streak" indicator.
- **Achievement Unlocks**: Add simple visual notifications when milestones are reached (e.g., "10 correct in a row").

## Content Expansion Requirements
### 1. Exercise Variety
Each unit must contain 50 exercises distributed among:
- **Vocabulary**: 15 exercises (Multiple choice, matching, gap fill).
- **Grammar**: 15 exercises (Conditionals, passive voice, modal verbs in negotiations).
- **Reading**: 10 exercises (Email analysis, contract snippets, strategic articles).
- **Listening/Speaking**: 10 exercises (Simulated dialogues, audio analysis, pronunciation).

### 2. Unit Structure (4 Units)
- **Unit 1**: The Preparation Phase (BATNA, Research, Objectives).
- **Unit 2**: Opening the Negotiation (Agenda, Tone, First Impressions).
- **Unit 3**: The Art of Bargaining (Concessions, Trade-offs, Leverage).
- **Unit 4**: Reaching Agreement & Closing (Consensus, Follow-up, Documentation).

## Success Metrics
- 100% completion of 200 exercises across 4 units.
- Noticeable improvement in UI/UX professional feel and responsiveness.
- All functional requirements (progress tracking, feedback) implemented.
