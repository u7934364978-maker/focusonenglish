# SEO Optimization Report: "CV en Inglés Ejemplos"

## What was implemented
1. **Title Optimization**: Changed the H1 and meta-title to lead with the primary keyword "CV en Inglés: Ejemplos, Guía Definitiva y Plantillas 2026".
2. **Search Intent Alignment**: Added a new section "Ejemplos de CV en Inglés por Perfiles" featuring 3 specific templates:
   - Modern & Tech (Resume format)
   - Classic & Professional (Management focus)
   - Junior/Student (Functional focus)
3. **Keyword Density**: Integrated synonyms and related terms like "plantillas", "CV vs Resume", and "ejemplos de currículum".
4. **Call-to-Action**: Added a dedicated section for template downloads and premium tool access to increase user engagement.
5. **Metadata Update**: Optimized the `excerpt` and `keywords` in the markdown frontmatter.

## How the solution was tested
- **Manual Review**: Verified the markdown structure and internal links.
- **Linting**: Ran `npm run lint` to ensure no breaking changes were introduced (existing codebase warnings unrelated to this task were present).
- **SEO Best Practices**: Applied standard On-Page SEO techniques (keyword placement in H1, first paragraph, and image alt text).

## Biggest challenges encountered
- **Balancing Content**: Ensuring the guide remained educational while adding enough "quick-win" examples to satisfy search intent.
- **Keyword Naturalness**: Integrating "ejemplos" frequently enough for Google without making the text feel repetitive for human readers.

## Future Recommendations
- **Rich Snippets**: Monitor Google Search Console to see if the FAQ schema is picked up.
- **Internal Linking**: Link to this guide from other popular blog posts related to "Career" or "Business English".
- **Visuals**: If possible, add high-quality screenshots of actual CV designs in the future to further satisfy visual intent.
