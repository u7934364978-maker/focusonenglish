# PRD: SEO Strategy for "aplicaciones para aprender ingles"

## Objective
Establish domain authority and rank for the high-volume keyword **"aplicaciones para aprender ingles"** (apps to learn English) by creating a high-quality pillar page and supporting content.

## Target Audience
- Spanish speakers looking for mobile or web tools to improve their English.
- Students and professionals seeking flexible learning methods.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Ranking in Top 10 for "aplicaciones para aprender ingles" within 3-6 months.
- Increase in organic traffic to the domain.
- Conversion rate from the pillar page to Focus English course signups.

## Content Strategy
1. **Pillar Page**: A comprehensive guide at `/aplicaciones-para-aprender-ingles` (or similar).
2. **Comparison Framework**: Objective comparison of popular apps (Duolingo, Babbel, ELSA Speak, etc.) vs. Focus English.
3. **Structured Data**: Implementation of `ItemList`, `SoftwareApplication`, and `FAQPage` schemas.
4. **Internal Linking**: Strategic links from the homepage and existing blog posts.

## Technical Requirements
- Creation of a new SEO-optimized page template or utilization of existing MD-based system.
- Support for complex layouts (comparison tables, feature lists).
- JSON-LD implementation for rich results.

## Assumptions
- We will prioritize organic authority over paid ads for this specific keyword.
- Focus English will be positioned as a "Premium Course with App-like flexibility" to differentiate.

## Unclear Aspects (Questions for User)
1. **URL Structure**: Should we use `/aplicaciones-para-aprender-ingles` or follow the current `/curso-ingles-x` pattern?
2. **Branding**: Should we include affiliate links for other apps or focus strictly on driving traffic to Focus English?
3. **Visuals**: Do we have screenshots or logos for the apps to be compared?
