# Product Requirements Document: SEO Domain Authority Strategy

## 1. Introduction
The goal is to establish Focus English as an authority in the specialized English learning niche (Work, Travel, Exams) by implementing a Topic Cluster strategy. This strategy will organize content around "Pillar Pages" supported by "Cluster Content" (blog posts) targeting specific keywords with lower difficulty and high relevance.

## 2. Goals
- Increase Domain Authority (DA) through a structured internal linking profile.
- Rank for long-tail keywords related to specialized English.
- Channel traffic from informational blog posts to high-converting course pages.

## 3. Keyword Categorization & Clustering
Based on the provided keyword list, the following clusters are identified:

### Cluster 1: English for Hospitality & Tourism
- **Pillar Page**: [English for Tourism and Hospitality](./src/content/cursos/viajes.md) (or similar)
- **Cluster Keywords**: 
    - `ingles para hosteleria`
    - `ingles para recepcionistas`
    - `ingles para camareros`
    - `cursos para trabajar en cruceros`
    - `como hacer una reservacion en ingles`
    - `frases para contestar el telefono`

### Cluster 2: Official Exam Preparation (Aptis, Cambridge, TOEFL)
- **Pillar Page**: [Official Exam Preparation Hub](./src/content/seo-pages/certificaciones-ingles.md)
- **Cluster Keywords**:
    - `examen first madrid`
    - `aptis a2`
    - `precio certificacion cambridge`
    - `toefl caducidad`
    - `reading aptis b1`
    - `cae speaking`

### Cluster 3: English for Work & Business
- **Pillar Page**: [English for Business/Work](./src/content/cursos/trabajo.md)
- **Cluster Keywords**:
    - `entrevista trabajo ingles`
    - `despedirse en un correo en ingles`
    - `formas de contestar el telefono en una empresa`
    - `ingles financiero`
    - `ejemplos de email en ingles`

### Cluster 4: Grammar & Language Skills (Supportive)
- **Pillar Page**: Grammar Hub / Blog Home
- **Cluster Keywords**:
    - `phrasal verbs b2`
    - `gramatica inglesa b1`
    - `condicionales ingles b2`
    - `voz pasiva en presente simple`
    - `animales en ingles`

## 4. Implementation Strategy
1. **Content Mapping**: Map each keyword to a new or existing blog post.
2. **Pillar Page Optimization**: Ensure pillar pages have high-quality content and link out to all supporting cluster posts.
3. **Internal Linking**: 
    - Every cluster post MUST link back to its Pillar Page with optimized anchor text.
    - Cluster posts should link to each other where relevant.
4. **Metadata & Technical SEO**: Ensure proper H1-H3 structure, meta descriptions, and alt tags for images in all new content.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Improvement in rankings for the targeted keywords.
- Increase in organic traffic to pillar pages.
- Reduction in bounce rate due to better internal navigation.
