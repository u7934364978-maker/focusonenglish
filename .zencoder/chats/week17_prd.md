# PRD: Week 17 - HR Operations: Recruitment & Onboarding (Expansion)

## Goal
Expand Week 17 of the B2 Administration course from 15 exercises to 45 exercises and from 4 theory sections to 8 theory sections.

## Content Strategy
The expansion will focus on advanced HR administration topics, including employee relations, performance management, and HR compliance.

### Block 2: Employee Relations & Engagement
- **Theory 5**: Handling Employee Grievances and Discipline.
- **Theory 6**: Employee Engagement and Retention Strategies.
- **Exercises 16-30**:
  - Identifying steps in a disciplinary procedure.
  - Vocabulary: *grievance*, *tribunal*, *mediation*, *attrition*.
  - Grammar: Practice with participle clauses for summarizing HR cases.

### Block 3: Performance Management & HR Compliance
- **Theory 7**: Performance Appraisals and KPIs.
- **Theory 8**: HR Compliance & Data Privacy (GDPR in HR).
- **Exercises 31-45**:
  - Drafting SMART goals for appraisals.
  - Identifying data privacy breaches in HR handling.
  - Listening placeholder for a performance review.
  - Writing tasks for summarizing meeting minutes or appraisal results.

## Technical Requirements
- Follow existing JSON schema for `theory.json` and `exercises.json`.
- Maintain exercise ID format: `adm-b2-w17-exXX`.
- Ensure all exercises have a `modelAnswer` or `answerIndex` and an `explanation` (where applicable).
