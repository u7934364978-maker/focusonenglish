# PRD: Week 15 - Budgeting & Financial Control for Admin (Expansion)

## Goal
Expand Week 15 of the B2 Administration course from 15 exercises to 45 exercises and from 4 theory sections to 8 theory sections.

## Content Strategy
The expansion will focus on advanced budgeting concepts and financial reporting, ensuring the content is practical for administrative professionals.

### Block 2: Budgeting Methodologies & Cost Allocation
- **Theory 5**: Zero-Based Budgeting (ZBB) vs. Incremental Budgeting.
- **Theory 6**: Cost Centers and Resource Allocation.
- **Exercises 16-30**:
  - Comparison of budgeting methods.
  - Identifying cost centers in a corporate structure.
  - Vocabulary: *discretionary spend*, *fixed costs*, *variable costs*.
  - Grammar: Practice with wishes and regrets in a financial context.

### Block 3: Financial Reporting, Audits & Cash Flow
- **Theory 7**: Financial Reporting & Internal Audits.
- **Theory 8**: Cash Flow Management for Admins (Petty cash, reconciliation).
- **Exercises 31-45**:
  - Reading an audit summary.
  - Petty cash reconciliation tasks.
  - Listening placeholder for a budget meeting.
  - Writing tasks for justifying expenditures.

## Technical Requirements
- Follow existing JSON schema for `theory.json` and `exercises.json`.
- Maintain exercise ID format: `adm-b2-w15-exXX`.
- Ensure all exercises have a `modelAnswer` or `answerIndex` and an `explanation` (where applicable).
