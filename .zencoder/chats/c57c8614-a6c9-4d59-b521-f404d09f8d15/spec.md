# Technical Specification - A1 Unit 1 Remake (Revised)

## Technical Context
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Framework**: Next.js 15
- **Data Store**: `src/lib/course/a1/unit-1.ts`

## Implementation Approach
We will rebuild `unit-1.ts` following a 3-act structure based on the Pedagogical Strategy in the PRD.

### Structure:
1.  **Act 1: The First Meeting (Exercises 1-20)**
    - *Context*: Sarah (UK) arrives at a new office and meets Mr. Brown (Formal) and then her colleague Alex (Informal).
    - *Topics*: Greetings, Farewells, Intro self/others, Verb 'To Be'.
2.  **Act 2: Getting to Know Each Other (Exercises 21-40)**
    - *Context*: Lunch break at the office. Characters discuss where they are from, their jobs, and ages.
    - *Topics*: Personal Info, Nationalities, Numbers, Wh-Questions, Possessive Adjectives.
3.  **Act 3: The Welcome Event (Exercises 41-60)**
    - *Context*: An evening social event. Characters exchange contact info and spell names for the guest list.
    - *Topics*: Alphabet/Spelling, Email addresses, Phone numbers, Review of all Unit 1 concepts.

## Delivery Phases
- **Phase 1**: Act 1 - The First Meeting (Ex 1-20)
- **Phase 2**: Act 2 - Getting to Know Each Other (Ex 21-40)
- **Phase 3**: Act 3 - The Welcome Event (Ex 41-60)
