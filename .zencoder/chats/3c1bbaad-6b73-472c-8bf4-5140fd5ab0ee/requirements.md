# PRD: SEO Strategy for "Cursos Online Inglés B1"

## 1. Goal
Establish high domain authority and top rankings for the keyword **"cursos online ingles b1"** (and related variants) through a comprehensive topical authority strategy.

## 2. Target Keywords
- **Primary**: `cursos online ingles b1`
- **Secondary**: 
  - `preparacion examen b1 ingles online`
  - `ejercicios ingles b1 online gratis`
  - `temario ingles b1 completo`
  - `mejor curso de ingles b1 online`
  - `clases de ingles b1 online`

## 3. SEO Strategy: Topical Authority (Hub & Spoke)
The strategy will follow a **Topic Cluster** model to signal to search engines that the site is an authority on the B1 level.

### 3.1. Pillar Page (The Hub)
- **URL**: `/cursos/ingles-b1` (or existing equivalent)
- **Content**: A comprehensive guide to the B1 level.
  - What is B1? (CEFR definition).
  - Importance of B1 for careers/education.
  - Full B1 Syllabus (Grammar, Vocabulary).
  - How our online course helps.
  - Testimonials/Success stories.
  - CTAs for enrollment.

### 3.2. Supporting Content (The Spokes)
Create highly optimized blog posts/guides that link back to the pillar page:
- "Guía Completa para el Examen B1 de Cambridge/Aptis"
- "Top 10 Errores Comunes en el Nivel B1 y Cómo Evitarlos"
- "Vocabulario Esencial para B1: Trabajo y Viajes"
- "Gramática B1: Dominando el Present Perfect y los Condicionales"
- "Recursos Gratuitos: Ejercicios Online de Inglés B1"

### 3.3. Internal Linking Strategy
- All supporting content must link to the Pillar Page using the primary keyword as anchor text.
- The Pillar Page must link to supporting content for detailed explanations.

## 4. Technical SEO Requirements
- **Schema Markup**: Implement `Course` and `FAQ` schema on the pillar page.
- **Performance**: Ensure Core Web Vitals are met (LCP < 2.5s).
- **Mobile Optimization**: Content must be perfectly readable on mobile.
- **Meta Tags**: Optimized Title tags (e.g., "Cursos Online Inglés B1: Consigue tu Certificado en 3 Meses") and Meta Descriptions.

## 5. Backlink Strategy (Off-page)
- **Guest Posting**: Identify 3-5 high-DA sites in the education/language niche for guest articles.
- **Resource Link Building**: Create a "B1 Exam Checklist PDF" that others would want to link to.

## 6. Success Metrics
- Position in SERPs for "cursos online ingles b1" (Target: Top 5).
- Organic traffic growth for B1-related pages.
- Number of internal links pointing to the B1 pillar page.
- Backlink profile growth (Referring domains).
