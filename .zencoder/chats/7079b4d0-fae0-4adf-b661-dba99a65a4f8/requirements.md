# PRD - Complete Unit 30 Exercises

## Goal
Complete the exercises for Unit 30 of the English A1 course, ensuring they follow the curriculum and pedagogical standards.

## Requirements
- Unit 30 must be a Review & Assessment of Module 3 (Units 21-29).
- Must cover: Rooms, Furniture, There is/are, Prepositions of Place, Neighborhood, Directions, Pets & Animals, Body Parts, Clothes.
- Must consist of approximately 60 exercises.
- EVERY English word in transcripts, questions, and reading passages must include its Spanish translation using the `[[English|Spanish]]` format.
- Exercises must progress from recognition to production.
- Include a Reading Comprehension section with a text and related questions.
- Maintain consistency with existing unit formats (JSON and TS).

## Verification
- JSON must be valid.
- TS must pass the project's validation tool (`scripts/validate-exercises.mjs`).
