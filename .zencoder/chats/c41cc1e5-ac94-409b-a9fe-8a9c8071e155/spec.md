# Pedagogical Specification: English A1 Course (60 Units)

## Pedagogical Framework: Communicative Language Teaching (CLT) & Task-Based Learning
The course follows a progression from **input recognition** to **structured production** and **communicative task completion**. Each unit follows a scaffolding approach where the 60 exercises move from simple identification to complex synthesis.

---

## 60-Unit Syllabus Structure

### Module 1: Foundations (Units 1-10)
1. **Greetings & Goodbyes**: Hello, Hi, Bye, See you. Verb 'To Be' (Positive).
2. **Alphabet & Spelling**: Personal names, common spelling patterns.
3. **Numbers 1-100**: Ages, phone numbers. Basic plurals.
4. **Introductions**: "I am...", "You are...". My/Your (Possessive adjectives).
5. **Nationalities & Countries**: "Where are you from?". "I am from...".
6. **Common Objects**: In the bag/room. This/That/These/Those.
7. **The Classroom**: Basic commands (Listen, Read, Write).
8. **Colors & Descriptions**: Basic adjectives (Big, Small, New, Old).
9. **Family Members**: Basic relations. 's (Possessive).
10. **Module 1 Review**: Consolidation of personal info and foundations.

### Module 2: Daily Life (Units 11-20)
11. **Telling the Time**: "What time is it?". Basic prepositions (At/In/On).
12. **Daily Routine (I)**: Wake up, eat, work. Present Simple (I, You, We, They).
13. **Daily Routine (II)**: Third person (He/She/It). -s/-es endings.
14. **Days of the Week & Months**: Appointments and schedules.
15. **My Home**: Rooms and furniture. There is / There are.
16. **Prepositions of Place**: In, On, Under, Next to.
17. **Frequency Adverbs**: Always, sometimes, never.
18. **Work & Professions**: "What do you do?". Indefinite articles (A/An).
19. **Workplace Tools**: Office supplies and equipment.
20. **Module 2 Review**: Daily life and work patterns.

### Module 3: Leisure & Social (Units 21-30)
21. **Hobbies & Interests**: Like/Love/Hate + -ing.
22. **Abilities**: Can/Can't for ability.
23. **Food & Drink (I)**: Common nouns. Countable/Uncountable basics.
24. **Food & Drink (II)**: Ordering at a cafÃ©. Some/Any.
25. **Sports & Exercise**: Go/Play/Do + Sport.
26. **Free Time Activities**: "What do you do on weekends?".
27. **Music & Entertainment**: Types of music/movies.
28. **Describing People (I)**: Physical appearance (Tall, short, hair).
29. **Describing People (II)**: Personality (Kind, funny, smart).
30. **Module 3 Review**: Socializing and personal expression.

### Module 4: Travel & Navigation (Units 31-40)
31. **The City**: Places in town (Bank, Hospital, Park).
32. **Giving Directions**: Left, right, straight on.
33. **Transport**: Bus, train, car, plane. "How do you get to...?".
34. **At the Airport**: Check-in, security, gate.
35. **The Hotel**: Checking in, facilities.
36. **Holiday Activities**: Sightseeing, relaxing. Present Continuous basics.
37. **Weather & Seasons**: "What's the weather like?".
38. **Clothes & Fashion**: Dressing for the occasion.
39. **Shopping (I)**: Prices, sizes, colors. "How much is...?".
40. **Module 4 Review**: Travel and navigation skills.

### Module 5: Health & Needs (Units 41-50)
41. **The Body**: Basic anatomy.
42. **Health Problems**: "I have a headache". Common ailments.
43. **Healthy Habits**: Food, sleep, exercise. Should/Shouldn't (Advice).
44. **Feelings & Emotions**: Happy, sad, tired, angry.
45. **Quantifiers**: Much, many, a lot of.
46. **Comparatives**: "X is better than Y". Adjective rules.
47. **Superlatives**: "The best/The biggest".
48. **Life Events**: Born, grew up, school. Past Simple 'To Be' (Was/Were).
49. **Memories**: "I was happy when...".
50. **Module 5 Review**: Health, comparisons, and past intro.

### Module 6: Future & Connections (Units 51-60)
51. **Past Simple (Regular)**: -ed endings. Pronunciation.
52. **Past Simple (Irregular)**: Went, ate, had, saw.
53. **Yesterday's Activities**: Describing recent past.
54. **Digital World**: Email, social media, internet.
55. **Future Plans (I)**: Going to (Intentions).
56. **Future Plans (II)**: Tomorrow, next week.
57. **Predictions**: Will/Won't (Basic).
58. **Final Vocabulary Boost**: Phrasal verbs intro (Get up, Look for).
59. **Course Integration**: Combining Past, Present, Future.
60. **Final Assessment**: A1 Level proficiency check.

## 60-Exercise Progression Template (Per Unit)

Each unit follows this sequence to ensure pedagogical depth:

### Set 1: Recognition & Foundation (10 Exercises)
- 1-4: **Visual Matching**: Match word to image.
- 5-7: **Audio Recognition**: Hear the word/phrase and select the image.
- 8-10: **Meaning Check**: Simple T/F or Multiple Choice on basic definitions.

### Set 2: Concept Acquisition (10 Exercises)
- 11-14: **Categorization**: Group words by type (e.g., verbs vs. nouns).
- 15-17: **Pattern Identification**: Spot the grammar rule in a short text.
- 18-20: **Logic Puzzles**: Match opposites or synonyms in context.

### Set 3: Controlled Practice (15 Exercises)
- 21-25: **Gap-fills (Word Bank)**: Complete sentences with provided options.
- 26-30: **Sentence Scrambling**: Reorder words to form correct sentences.
- 31-35: **Negative & Question Transformation**: Change positive sentences.

### Set 4: Functional Communication (10 Exercises)
- 36-40: **Dialogue Completion**: Choose the best response in a conversation.
- 41-45: **Situational Matching**: Match a phrase to its social purpose (e.g., "Bless you" -> after a sneeze).

### Set 5: Active Production (10 Exercises)
- 46-50: **Transcription**: Listen and type exactly what you hear.
- 51-55: **Guided Translation**: Translate key phrases from L1 to English.

### Set 6: Synthesis & Mastery (5 Exercises)
- 56-58: **Error Correction**: Find and fix the mistake in a sentence.
- 59-60: **Mini-Challenge**: Fast-paced recall or context-heavy synthesis.
