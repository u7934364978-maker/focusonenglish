# PRD - SEO Article: "clases de ingles"

## 1. Overview
The goal is to create a high-quality, SEO-optimized blog article for the Focus English platform, targeting the primary keyword **"clases de ingles"**. The article should align with the brand's tone and current blog structure.

## 2. Target Audience
- Professionals looking to improve their English for work.
- Travelers needing basic to intermediate English.
- Students preparing for English exams.
- Anyone interested in online English learning.

## 3. SEO Requirements
- **Primary Keyword**: "clases de ingles"
- **Secondary Keywords**: (To be researched/defined, e.g., "clases de inglés online", "aprender inglés", "mejores clases de inglés")
- **Meta Description**: Engaging excerpt including the primary keyword.
- **Title Tag**: Catchy title including the primary keyword, optimized for CTR.
- **Structure**: Use H1, H2, and H3 tags correctly.
- **Internal Linking**: Link to Focus English features (AI simulator, courses, etc.).
- **External Linking**: Link to authoritative sources if relevant.

## 4. Content Requirements
- **Tone**: Professional, encouraging, and authoritative.
- **Format**: Markdown with YAML frontmatter.
- **Frontmatter Fields**:
    - `title`: SEO-optimized title.
    - `date`: Current date.
    - `author`: "Focus English Team".
    - `excerpt`: Meta description.
    - `category`: To be decided (likely "general" or a new category if appropriate, but "trabajo" or "viajes" are existing ones).
    - `readTime`: Estimated reading time.
    - `image`: Relevant Unsplash image URL.
    - `keywords`: List of SEO keywords.
    - `faqs`: 3-5 relevant FAQs.
- **Length**: ~1000-1500 words.

## 5. Unclear Aspects & Decisions
- **Category**: The article will be placed in the `metodos` category (Métodos de Aprendizaje). This aligns with the existing category metadata in the app.
- **Location**: `src/content/blog/metodos/clases-de-ingles-guia.md`.
- **Specific Angle**: A comprehensive guide titled "Clases de Inglés: Guía para elegir la mejor opción en 2026", focusing on different types of classes and why Focus English's AI-driven approach is effective.

## 6. Success Metrics
- Search engine ranking for "clases de ingles".
- User engagement (time on page).
- Conversion to sign-up/free trial.
