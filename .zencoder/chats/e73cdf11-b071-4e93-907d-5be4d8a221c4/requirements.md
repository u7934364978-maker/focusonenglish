# PRD - Content Creation: 10 Educational English Articles

## Overview
The goal is to create 10 high-quality, educational blog articles in Spanish to improve SEO and provide value to users of Focus English. These articles will be divided into two main categories: Travel and Work.

## Requirements
- **Total Articles**: 10
- **Categories**: 
    - 5 Articles about **Travel** (viajes)
    - 5 Articles about **English at Work** (trabajo)
- **Primary Keyword**: "clases de ingl√©s" must be naturally integrated into each article.
- **Language**: Spanish.
- **Tone**: Creative, educational, and professional.
- **Format**: Markdown with YAML frontmatter, following the existing site structure.

## Content Structure (per article)
- **Title**: Engaging and SEO-friendly.
- **Date**: Current date (2026-02-01).
- **Author**: "Focus English Team".
- **Excerpt**: Short summary for the blog list view.
- **Category**: "viajes" or "trabajo".
- **Read Time**: Estimated reading time (e.g., "8 min").
- **Image**: Unsplash URL or relevant placeholder.
- **Keywords**: List of relevant keywords including the primary one.
- **FAQs**: At least 3 frequently asked questions with answers in the frontmatter.
- **Body**: Structured with headers (H2, H3), lists, and bold text for emphasis.
