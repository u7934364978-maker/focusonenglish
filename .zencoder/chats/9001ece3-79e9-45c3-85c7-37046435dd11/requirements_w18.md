# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Week 18 Expansion

## 1. Feature Overview
Expand the content for Week 18 of the B2 Administration course (HR Operations: Performance Reviews & Feedback). Currently, the week has 1 block of 15 exercises. This task adds 4 more blocks of 15 exercises each, bringing the total to 75 exercises, along with corresponding theory for the new blocks.

## 2. Target Audience
B2 level English learners focusing on administrative roles in Human Resources.

## 3. Content Requirements

### Block 2: Managing Underperformance & PIPs
- **Theory**: Formal procedures for addressing performance issues, drafting a PIP, and documenting progress.
- **Exercises**: 15 items (Ex 16-30).
- **Grammar**: Continue with Cleft Sentences.

### Block 3: Rewards, Recognition, and Promotions
- **Theory**: Administrative side of employee rewards, managing promotion cycles, and internal job postings.
- **Exercises**: 15 items (Ex 31-45).

### Block 4: Diversity, Inclusion, and Equality (DEI)
- **Theory**: Implementing DEI policies, reporting on workforce diversity, and ensuring inclusive administrative processes.
- **Exercises**: 15 items (Ex 46-60).

### Block 5: Health, Safety, and Employee Wellbeing
- **Theory**: Workplace safety audits, risk assessments, and wellness program administration.
- **Exercises**: 15 items (Ex 61-75).

### General Constraints
- **Exercise Types**: multipleChoice, writingTask, reading-comprehension.
- **Grammar focus**: Cleft sentences (It-clefts and What-clefts) should be reinforced throughout.
- **Language**: British/International English.

- Unique and sequential IDs (adm-b2-w18-exXX).
