# Requirements - New Blog Article: English for Negotiations

## Goal
Establish authority in high-level business interactions, specifically focusing on negotiation tactics, persuasion, and closing deals in English.

## Target Keywords
- "negociaciones en inglés"
- "frases para negociar en inglés"
- "vocabulario de negocios inglés"
- "técnicas de negociación inglés"
- "cerrar tratos en inglés"

## Content Structure
1. **Introduction**: The psychology of negotiation and the importance of precise language.
2. **Setting the Stage**: Phrases for starting the negotiation and stating your position.
3. **The Art of Bargaining**: "If... then..." structures (conditionals), making concessions, and handling objections.
4. **Clarification**: Ensuring both parties are on the same page (avoiding misunderstandings).
5. **Closing the Deal**: Confirming agreements, next steps, and professional goodbyes.
6. **Cultural Nuances**: Direct vs. Indirect styles (US/UK vs. other cultures).
7. **Focus English Tip**: Using AI to simulate high-pressure negotiation scenarios.

## Tone and Style
- Strategic, sophisticated, and professional.
- 2026 global business context.
- Spanish language.
