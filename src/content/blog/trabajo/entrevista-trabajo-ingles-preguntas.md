## Prepara tu Entrevista de Trabajo en Inglés como un Profesional

Una entrevista de trabajo en inglés es un reto doble: no solo debes demostrar tus competencias profesionales, sino hacerlo en un idioma que quizás no es tu lengua materna. La buena noticia es que con una preparación estructurada, puedes llegar a la entrevista con total confianza.

Esta guía recoge las **30 preguntas más frecuentes** en entrevistas internacionales, explicadas con estrategia de respuesta y ejemplos reales.

---

## 🎯 El Método STAR: Tu Arma Secreta

Antes de las preguntas, necesitas conocer el **método STAR**, la estructura de oro para responder cualquier pregunta conductual (behavioral question):

- **S**ituation: Describe el contexto brevemente.
- **T**ask: ¿Cuál era tu responsabilidad o el problema a resolver?
- **A**ction: ¿Qué hiciste exactamente tú?
- **R**esult: ¿Cuál fue el resultado, preferiblemente cuantificable?

---

## 🔑 Preguntas de Apertura: Rompe el Hielo con Impacto

### 1. "Tell me about yourself."
**Estrategia:** No es una invitación a contar tu vida. Es un pitch profesional de 60-90 segundos.

> *"I'm a digital marketing specialist with 6 years of experience in B2B SaaS companies. In my current role at [Company], I led the content strategy that grew organic traffic by 80% in 18 months. I'm now looking for a senior role where I can build and mentor a team, which is why this position at [Company] is particularly exciting to me."*

### 2. "Why do you want to work here?"
**Estrategia:** Investiga la empresa antes. Menciona algo específico: su misión, un producto, su cultura o un proyecto reciente.

> *"I've been following [Company]'s work in sustainable logistics for two years, and your recent partnership with [X] confirms that you're genuinely committed to reducing supply chain emissions, not just talking about it. My background in operations and my passion for sustainability make this an ideal fit."*

### 3. "What are your strengths?"
**Estrategia:** Menciona 2-3 fortalezas relevantes para el puesto y respalda cada una con un ejemplo breve.

> *"One of my core strengths is data analysis. In my last role, I identified a pattern in our churn data that nobody had spotted before, which led to a product change that improved retention by 15%."*

## 💼 Preguntas sobre Experiencia y Logros

### 4. "Walk me through your CV / Resume."
Diferente a "Tell me about yourself" — aquí quieren cronología. Sé conciso: 2-3 frases por etapa clave.

### 5. "What is your greatest professional achievement?"
Usa el método STAR. Elige un logro con resultados medibles y relevante para el puesto.

### 6. "Describe a challenging project and how you handled it."
> *"When I joined [Company], the team had just lost its main client, which accounted for 30% of revenue. I immediately mapped our remaining client relationships and proposed a retention strategy that included quarterly business reviews and customized success plans. Within 6 months, we had retained 95% of the remaining portfolio and signed two new accounts."*

### 7. "Have you ever had to meet a tight deadline? How did you manage it?"
Ejemplo de estructura:
> *"Yes. [Situation]. My task was [Task]. I [Action: prioritize, delegate, communicate]. The result was [Result]."*

### 8. "Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned."
No evites esta pregunta. Los reclutadores buscan autoconciencia (self-awareness) y capacidad de aprendizaje.

## 🤝 Preguntas sobre Trabajo en Equipo y Liderazgo

### 9. "Describe your leadership style."
> *"I'd describe my style as collaborative but decisive. I make sure every voice is heard during the planning phase, but when it's time to move, I take ownership of the direction and ensure the team has clarity on priorities."*

### 10. "Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a colleague."
Enfócate en la resolución, no en el problema. Nunca hables mal de nadie.

## 🧠 Preguntas de Comportamiento y Situacionales

### 14. "How do you handle pressure and stress?"
> *"I try to see pressure as a signal to prioritize ruthlessly. When a major campaign launch clashed with a product incident at my last job, I quickly assessed what was time-sensitive vs. what could wait 48 hours, communicated clearly with both teams, and we delivered both without major issues."*

### 15. "Describe a time you had to adapt to a significant change."
### 16. "Give an example of a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information."
### 17. "Tell me about a time you went above and beyond."
### 18. "How do you prioritize your work when you have multiple deadlines?"

**Useful phrases:**
- *"I use a combination of urgency and impact to prioritize..."*
- *"I communicate proactively if I foresee a bottleneck..."*

## 💰 Preguntas sobre Motivación y Expectativas

### 19. "What are your salary expectations?"
> *"Based on my research on market rates for this role in [City] and my experience, I'm looking in the range of [X-Y]. That said, I'm open to discussing the full compensation package."*

### 20. "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"
Alinéalo con el crecimiento en la empresa, sin sonar como si planearas irte pronto.

### 21. "Why are you leaving your current job?"
Frases positivas y profesionales:
- *"I'm looking for a role with greater scope for impact."*
- *"I've learned a lot, but I feel ready for the next challenge."*
- Nunca: *"My boss is terrible"* o *"The company is a mess."*

### 22. "Are you considering other offers?"
Sé honesto pero sin dar detalles: *"Yes, I'm in conversations with a couple of companies, but this role is my top priority."*

## 🎯 Las Preguntas que Más Sorprenden a Candidatos

### 23. "What's your biggest weakness?"
Elige una debilidad real pero no crítica para el puesto, y siempre añade lo que estás haciendo para mejorarla.
> *"I tend to over-prepare for presentations, which sometimes takes longer than ideal. I've been working on trusting my expertise more and using structured templates to speed up the process."*

### 24. "If you were an animal, which would you be and why?" (Preguntas creativas)
Estas preguntas miden creatividad y autoconocimiento. No hay respuesta incorrecta si la justificas bien.

### 25. "What do you know about our competitors?"
Investiga 2-3 competidores principales antes de la entrevista.

### 26. "Sell me this pen." (Para roles de ventas)
Enfócate en hacer preguntas primero para entender las necesidades, luego presenta el valor.

---

## ❓ Las Preguntas QUE DEBES HACER TÚ al Final

Una entrevista es bidireccional. Siempre prepara 3-5 preguntas:

- *"What does success look like in this role after the first 90 days?"*
- *"What are the biggest challenges the team is currently facing?"*
- *"How would you describe the team culture?"*
- *"What opportunities are there for professional development?"*
- *"What are the next steps in the process?"*
