  - question: ¿Cómo digo "en este momento no puedo ayudarte con eso" en inglés de forma profesional?
    answer: >-
      La forma más profesional es redirigir positivamente: "That's outside my
      area, but let me connect you with the right team who can definitely help
      you with that." O si es una política: "I understand what you're looking
      for; however, our current policy doesn't allow for that. What I can do

  - question: ¿Cómo expreso empatía en inglés sin que suene falso o exagerado?
    answer: >-
      La clave es la especificidad. En lugar de "I understand" (genérico), di
      "I understand how inconvenient it must have been to wait three weeks for
      a delivery that never arrived." Referenciar el problema específico del
      cliente hace que la empatía suene auténtica.
  - question: ¿Qué diferencia hay entre "customer" y "client" en inglés?
    answer: >-
      "Customer" es más general y se usa para compras transaccionales (tiendas,
      restaurantes, e-commerce). "Client" implica una relación más formal y
      continuada (abogados, consultores, agencias). En el ámbito del customer
      service, "customer" es más frecuente, aunque "client" se usa en servicios
      profesionales B2B.

## El Banco de Frases que Todo Profesional de Customer Service Necesita

Dominar las **frases de inglés para atención al cliente** te permite gestionar cualquier situación con profesionalidad y empatía, independientemente de tu nivel general de inglés. Esta colección de 50 expresiones está organizada por situación para que puedas encontrar la frase correcta en el momento en que la necesitas.

Para estrategias más profundas de comunicación en inglés para customer service, consulta nuestra **[guía completa de inglés para atención al cliente](/blog/trabajo/ingles-customer-service-telefono-chat)**.

---

## 👋 Grupo 1: Bienvenida y Apertura (Frases 1-8)

1. *"Thank you for contacting [Company]. My name is [Name]. How can I assist you today?"*

2. *"Welcome to [Company] support! I'm [Name] and I'll be happy to help you. What brings you in today?"*

3. *"Good [morning/afternoon], [Name]. Thank you for reaching out. I can see from your account that you [context]. Is that what you're calling about?"*

4. *"I appreciate you taking the time to contact us. I'm here to help, so let's get this sorted out for you."*

5. *"Hi [Name]! I see you've been a customer with us for [X years] — thank you for your loyalty! How can I help you today?"*

6. *"I understand you've been experiencing some difficulty. I'm sorry to hear that, and I want to make sure we resolve this for you."*

7. *"Before we dive in, could I ask for your account number or the email address on your account? Just so I can pull up your details."*

8. *"Just to let you know, this conversation may be recorded for quality and training purposes. Is that okay?"*

---

## 🔍 Grupo 2: Recopilar Información y Confirmar (Frases 9-16)

9. *"Could you give me a bit more detail about the issue you're experiencing?"*

10. *"When did this first happen? And has it happened before?"*

11. *"So, just to make sure I've understood correctly — [paraphrase the issue]. Is that right?"*

12. *"And what were you expecting to happen instead?"*

13. *"Could you read me the order number / reference number / error message?"*

14. *"What troubleshooting steps, if any, have you already tried?"*

15. *"Is this affecting just you, or other members of your team as well?"*

16. *"Thank you for explaining that so clearly. I have a full picture of the situation now."*

---

## ⏱️ Grupo 3: Gestión de Tiempos de Espera (Frases 17-22)

17. *"I'm going to check on this for you right now. Please bear with me for just a moment."*

18. *"I appreciate your patience. I'm still looking into this."*

19. *"I need to place you on a brief hold — it should only be about two minutes. Is that okay with you?"*

20. *"Thank you so much for waiting. I have the information you need."*

21. *"I'm sorry for the delay. This is taking slightly longer than expected, but I want to make sure I give you the correct information."*

22. *"Rather than keeping you on hold, I can research this and call you back within [timeframe]. Would that work for you?"*

---

## ✅ Grupo 4: Resolución Positiva (Frases 23-30)

23. *"Great news! I've been able to resolve this on my end. Here's what I've done..."*

24. *"I've processed [refund/replacement/cancellation] for you. You should see this reflected in [timeframe]."*

25. *"I can confirm that [solution]. You'll receive an email confirmation within the next few minutes."*

26. *"I'm happy to report that this has now been resolved. Is there anything else I can help you with?"*

27. *"I've made a note on your account so that if you need to contact us again, any agent can see exactly what we agreed today."*

28. *"Just to summarize what we've done today: [1, 2, 3]. Does that all sound correct?"*

29. *"I'm going to stay on the line until you can confirm this is working. Take your time."*

30. *"I'm really glad we were able to sort that out for you. Thank you for your patience!"*

---

## 😤 Grupo 5: Gestión de Quejas y Clientes Difíciles (Frases 31-38)

31. *"I completely understand your frustration, and I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience this has caused."*

32. *"You're absolutely right to be disappointed. This is not the standard of service we aim to provide."*

33. *"I want to assure you that I'm going to do everything I can to fix this for you."*

34. *"I hear you. Let's focus on finding the best solution for you right now."*

35. *"I understand you've had to contact us [multiple times] about this, and I can imagine how frustrating that must be. Let me make this the last time you need to."*

36. *"I'm sorry if a previous agent wasn't able to resolve this. I'm going to take ownership of this issue personally."*

37. *"I want to be fully transparent with you: [honest explanation of the situation]."*

38. *"I understand this may not be the answer you were hoping for, but I want to be honest rather than make promises I can't keep."*

---

## 🔄 Grupo 6: Devoluciones, Reembolsos y Cambios (Frases 39-44)

39. *"I'd be happy to process a return for you. Just to let you know our returns policy: [brief explanation]."*

40. *"I can offer you a full refund, or if you'd prefer, a store credit for [amount]. Which would you like?"*

41. *"We'll arrange for a free collection of the item from your address. Could you confirm your postcode?"*

42. *"Your refund will be processed within [X] business days and will be returned to the original payment method."*

43. *"I can send you a replacement free of charge. Could you confirm your delivery address?"*

44. *"As a gesture of goodwill for the inconvenience caused, I'd like to offer you [discount/credit/upgrade]."*

---

## 🌟 Grupo 7: Cierre y Fidelización (Frases 45-50)

45. *"Is there anything else I can help you with today before we end the call/chat?"*

46. *"Thank you so much for your patience and for giving us the opportunity to resolve this."*

47. *"We truly value your business, and I hope today's experience reflects the high standard of service we aim for."*

48. *"If you have any further questions, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're available [hours/channels]."*

49. *"You'll receive a brief survey about today's interaction. Your feedback helps us improve, so I'd really appreciate it if you could take a moment to complete it."*

50. *"Thank you for being a [Company] customer. Have a wonderful day!"*
