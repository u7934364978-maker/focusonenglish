## Frases para la Entrevista de Trabajo en Inglés: Habla con Naturalidad

Conocer la gramática está bien. Pero en una entrevista de trabajo en inglés, lo que marca la diferencia es tener **frases preparadas y automatizadas** para cada situación. Este artículo es tu arsenal de expresiones profesionales organizadas por momentos clave de la entrevista.

---

## 👋 Frases para los Primeros Minutos: Saludo y Presentación

El inicio de la entrevista marca el tono. Practica estas frases hasta que salgan de forma natural:

| Situación | Frase en inglés |
|:----------|:----------------|
| Saludar al llegar | *"Good morning / afternoon. It's great to meet you."* |
| Agradecer la oportunidad | *"Thank you so much for having me today."* |
| Si han tenido que esperar | *"I hope I haven't kept you waiting."* |
| Romper el hielo | *"I really enjoyed learning about [Company] while preparing for today."* |
| Responder a "How are you?" | *"I'm doing very well, thank you. Excited to be here."* |

## 🎤 Frases para Hablar de Tu Perfil Profesional

### Para tu presentación inicial ("Tell me about yourself"):
- *"I'm a [job title] with [X] years of experience in [sector]."*
- *"Throughout my career, I've focused on [core area], with a particular strength in [specialty]."*
- *"Most recently, I was at [Company], where I [key achievement]."*
- *"I'm now looking for an opportunity where I can [what you want to do], which is why this role at [Company] really stood out to me."*

### Para hablar de logros:
- *"In my previous role, I successfully [action + result]."*
- *"One of my proudest achievements was [achievement]. We managed to [measurable result]."*
- *"I was instrumental in [project], which led to [outcome]."*
- *"Under my leadership, the team [achieved X] within [timeframe]."*

---

## 💪 Frases para Hablar de Fortalezas y Debilidades

### Fortalezas:
- *"One of my key strengths is [strength]. For example, [brief story]."*
- *"Colleagues often describe me as [adjective]. I think that's because [reason]."*
- *"I pride myself on my ability to [skill], especially in [context]."*

### Debilidades (con la estructura problema + solución):
- *"I've noticed I tend to [weakness]. To address this, I've been [action you're taking]."*
- *"I'm a perfectionist, which sometimes means I spend more time on details than necessary. I've been working on trusting my team more and setting clearer 'good enough' benchmarks."*
- *"Public speaking used to be a challenge for me. I took a presentation skills course last year, and I've been volunteering to lead more team meetings since then."*

## 🧠 Frases para Preguntas Conductuales (Behavioral Questions)

Las preguntas que empiezan con "Tell me about a time when..." o "Give me an example of..." requieren el método STAR:

### Para introducir tu historia:
- *"Sure. Let me give you a specific example."*
- *"This actually happened at [Company] when..."*
- *"A good example of this would be..."*

### Para describir la situación:
- *"At the time, our team was facing [challenge]."*
- *"The context was that [background information]."*
- *"We were under pressure to [objective]."*

### Para describir tu acción:
- *"I decided to [action]."*
- *"My approach was to first [step 1], then [step 2]."*
- *"I took the initiative to [proactive action]."*
- *"I collaborated closely with [stakeholders] to [goal]."*

### Para presentar el resultado:
- *"As a result, we [outcome]."*
- *"This led to [result], which represented a [X%] improvement."*
- *"The project was delivered on time / under budget / above expectations."*
- *"More importantly, it taught me [lesson]."*

## 🤝 Frases para Demostrar Encaje Cultural y Motivación

- *"What excites me most about this role is [specific aspect]."*
- *"I'm particularly drawn to [Company] because of [culture, mission, product]."*
- *"I believe my background in [area] aligns well with what you're looking for."*
- *"I see this as a natural next step in my career because [reason]."*
- *"The opportunity to work with [team / technology / market] is something I've been actively seeking."*

---

## 💰 Frases para Hablar de Salario y Condiciones

- *"Based on my research and experience, I'm targeting a range of [X to Y]."*
- *"I'm open to discussing the full compensation package, including benefits and flexibility."*
- *"What is the budget range for this position?"*
- *"Is there flexibility on the salary side?"*
- *"I'd be happy to discuss this further once we're both clear on the full scope of the role."*

---

## ❓ Frases para Hacer Tus Preguntas al Final

Recuerda: siempre lleva preguntas preparadas. Aquí tienes opciones para distintos enfoques:

**Sobre el rol:**
- *"What does success look like in the first 90 days?"*
- *"What are the biggest priorities for this role in the short term?"*

**Sobre el equipo:**
- *"Can you tell me more about the team I'd be working with?"*
- *"How does this team collaborate with other departments?"*

**Sobre la empresa:**
- *"How would you describe the company culture?"*
- *"What do you enjoy most about working here?"*

**Sobre los próximos pasos:**
- *"What are the next steps in the hiring process?"*
- *"When can I expect to hear back from you?"*

## 🆘 Frases de Rescate: Qué Decir Cuando No Sabes la Respuesta

Todos nos quedamos en blanco alguna vez. Estas frases te sacan de apuros de forma profesional:

| Situación | Frase |
|:----------|:------|
| Ganar tiempo | *"That's a really thoughtful question. Let me take a moment to think about that."* |
| No entendiste la pregunta | *"Could you clarify what you mean by [term]?"* |
| Quieres asegurarte de responder bien | *"Just to make sure I'm answering the right thing — are you asking about [X] or [Y]?"* |
| No tienes experiencia directa | *"I haven't faced that exact situation, but something similar was [example] and I handled it by..."* |
| Necesitas reconsiderar | *"Actually, let me rephrase that..."* |

## 🏁 Frases para Cerrar la Entrevista con Fuerza

El cierre es tan importante como la apertura. Deja una impresión memorable:

- *"Thank you so much for your time today. I really enjoyed our conversation."*
- *"I'm even more excited about this role after our discussion."*
- *"I believe my experience in [area] would allow me to make an immediate impact here."*
- *"I look forward to the next steps."*
- *"Is there anything else you'd like to know about my background?"*

## 📋 Vocabulario Profesional de Alto Impacto

Evita palabras genéricas y usa estas alternativas más potentes:

| Evita | Usa en su lugar |
|:------|:----------------|
| good | excellent, outstanding, exceptional |
| help | support, assist, facilitate, empower |
| make | develop, build, implement, establish |
| problem | challenge, obstacle, complexity |
| responsible for | oversaw, led, managed, spearheaded |
| used | leveraged, utilized, applied |
| very | highly, significantly, considerably |
