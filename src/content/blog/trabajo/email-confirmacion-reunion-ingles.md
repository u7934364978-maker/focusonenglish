Las reuniones en entornos internacionales se gestionan casi siempre por email antes de que nadie entre a una sala o a un Zoom. Saber cómo convocar, confirmar y cancelar con las fórmulas adecuadas en inglés es una competencia práctica que se nota en el día a día.

## Convocar una reunión

### El asunto

El asunto de una convocatoria de reunión debe incluir el tipo de reunión y el tema:

- *Meeting Request: Q2 Budget Review*
- *Team Sync — [Nombre del Proyecto] — 15 March*
- *Can We Schedule a Call This Week? [Tema]*

### El cuerpo del email de convocatoria

La estructura básica:
1. Razón de la reunión
2. Fecha/hora propuesta (con zona horaria si es necesario)
3. Duración estimada
4. Lugar o enlace virtual
5. Agenda (si la tienes)

**Frases para abrir:**
- *I would like to schedule a meeting to discuss [tema].*
- *I am writing to arrange a call at your earliest convenience.*
- *Could we find a time this week to go over [tema]?*

**Para proponer fechas y horas:**
- *Would you be available on [día] at [hora] CET?*
- *I have two slots available: [opción 1] or [opción 2]. Please let me know which works best.*
- *If neither of those times works, please feel free to suggest an alternative.*

**Para especificar duración y formato:**
- *I expect the meeting to last approximately [tiempo].*
- *This will be a brief 30-minute call.*
- *We could meet in person or via Zoom — whatever is more convenient for you.*

### Ejemplo de convocatoria

> **Subject:** Meeting Request: Product Launch Timeline Review
>
> Dear Team,
>
> I would like to schedule a meeting to review the timeline for the Q3 product launch and align on next steps.
>
> I am available on Tuesday 18 March at 10:00 AM CET or Thursday 20 March at 2:00 PM CET. The meeting should take approximately 45 minutes.
>
> We will cover the following:
> - Current status of each workstream
> - Dependencies and blockers
> - Revised timeline and owner confirmation
>
> Please let me know which slot works for you, or suggest an alternative if neither is suitable.
>
> Kind regards,
> Roberto Méndez

## Confirmar asistencia a una reunión

Respuestas de confirmación:

- *Thank you for the invitation. I confirm my attendance on [fecha] at [hora].*
- *I will be there. Looking forward to it.*
- *This time works perfectly for me. I have added the meeting to my calendar.*
- *Confirmed. I'll send through the agenda beforehand.*

Si necesitas confirmar con alguna reserva:

- *I can make it, but I may need to leave by [hora] due to another commitment.*
- *I'll join online if that's an option — I'll be traveling that day.*

## Pedir cambio de horario

La clave: avisar pronto y ofrecer alternativas en el mismo mensaje.

- *I'm afraid I have a conflict at [hora]. Would it be possible to reschedule?*
- *Something urgent has come up. Could we move the meeting to [alternativa]?*
- *I can no longer make [hora] on [día]. I have availability at [opción 1] or [opción 2] — please let me know if either works.*

Si es el organizador quien quiere cambiar:
- *I need to reschedule our meeting on [fecha]. I apologize for any inconvenience.*
- *Could we move our [hora] call to [nueva fecha]? I have the following slots available: [opciones].*

## Cancelar una reunión

Cancelar requiere: disculpa, razón breve y, si es posible, propuesta de nueva fecha.

**Cancelación con reprogramación:**
- *I am afraid I need to cancel our meeting scheduled for [fecha]. I apologize for the short notice. Could we reschedule for [alternativa]?*
- *Unfortunately, I will not be able to attend our call on [fecha] due to [razón breve]. I'd like to propose [nueva fecha] instead — please let me know if that works.*

**Cancelación definitiva:**
- *I am writing to let you know that the meeting scheduled for [fecha] has been cancelled.*
- *After careful consideration, we have decided to cancel the meeting. Thank you for your time.*
- *The [reunión] has been postponed until further notice. I will be in touch when we have a new date confirmed.*

## Gestionar respuestas y confirmaciones

Cuando recibes confirmaciones de varios asistentes:
- *Thank you all for confirming. I will send the final agenda and dial-in details tomorrow.*
- *Great — we're all confirmed for [fecha/hora]. I'll circulate the meeting notes afterwards.*

Cuando no recibes respuesta:
- *Just a quick follow-up on my meeting request from [fecha]. Could you let me know whether the proposed time works for you?*
- *I wanted to check in on the meeting invitation I sent on [fecha]. Please let me know if you need a different time slot.*

Gestionar reuniones con claridad en inglés evita malentendidos, ahorra tiempo y proyecta profesionalidad. Unas pocas fórmulas memorizadas cubren el 80% de las situaciones que te vas a encontrar.
