# A2 Premium Course Syllabus (60 Units)

## Module 1: People & Daily Life (Units 1-10)
- **Unit 1**: Greetings & Personal Info (A2 level review & expansion)
- **Unit 2**: Describing Appearance (Tall, slim, curly hair, etc.)
- **Unit 3**: Personality & Character (Kind, lazy, outgoing, etc.)
- **Unit 4**: Family & Relationships (Relative types, marital status)
- **Unit 5**: Daily Routines & Habits (Review + 'usually', 'hardly ever')
- **Unit 6**: Housework & Home Activities (Cleaning, cooking, laundry)
- **Unit 7**: Jobs & The Workplace (Office life, common professions)
- **Unit 8**: School & University Life (Subjects, exams, grades)
- **Unit 9**: Feelings & Emotions (Happy, frustrated, excited)
- **Unit 10**: Module 1 Review & Assessment

## Module 2: The World Around Us (Units 11-20)
- **Unit 11**: City Life & Places (Bank, library, town hall)
- **Unit 12**: Shopping & Department Stores (Sections, prices, paying)
- **Unit 13**: Food, Cooking & Recipes (Ingredients, instructions)
- **Unit 14**: At the Restaurant (Ordering, complaining, tipping)
- **Unit 15**: Sports & Fitness (Play, do, go + sports equipment)
- **Unit 16**: Health & Common Illnesses (Headache, flu, medicine)
- **Unit 17**: The Natural World & Animals (Wild animals, landscapes)
- **Unit 18**: Weather & Seasons (Forecasts, extreme weather)
- **Unit 19**: Clothes, Styles & Materials (Cotton, striped, smart)
- **Unit 20**: Module 2 Review & Assessment

## Module 3: Past & Memories (Units 21-30)
- **Unit 21**: Past Simple: To Be & Regular Verbs (Review)
- **Unit 22**: Past Simple: Irregular Verbs (Common irregulars)
- **Unit 23**: Past Simple: Questions & Negatives (Did you...? I didn't...)
- **Unit 24**: A Day to Remember (Narrating a specific past event)
- **Unit 25**: Childhood & School Days (Memories of growing up)
- **Unit 26**: Past Continuous: Setting the Scene (I was walking...)
- **Unit 27**: Past Simple vs Past Continuous (Interrupted actions)
- **Unit 28**: Important Dates & Years (Historical events, centuries)
- **Unit 29**: Famous People & Biographies (Life stories)
- **Unit 30**: Module 3 Review & Assessment

## Module 4: Future & Plans (Units 31-40)
- **Unit 31**: Future with 'Going to': Intentions (I'm going to travel)
- **Unit 32**: Future with 'Present Continuous': Arrangements (I'm meeting...)
- **Unit 33**: Future with 'Will': Predictions (I think it will rain)
- **Unit 34**: Future with 'Will': Offers & Promises (I'll help you)
- **Unit 35**: Travel Plans & Bookings (Tickets, hotels, itineraries)
- **Unit 36**: Weekend & Holiday Plans (What are you doing?)
- **Unit 37**: Future Hopes & Dreams (Ambitions)
- **Unit 38**: Time Clauses: When, As soon as (Future meaning)
- **Unit 39**: Zero Conditional: General Truths (If you heat water...)
- **Unit 40**: Module 4 Review & Assessment

## Module 5: Experiences & Comparisons (Units 41-50)
- **Unit 41**: Present Perfect: Life Experiences (I have been to...)
- **Unit 42**: Present Perfect: Ever & Never (Questions about experiences)
- **Unit 43**: Present Perfect: Just, Already, Yet (Recent actions)
- **Unit 44**: Present Perfect: For & Since (Duration)
- **Unit 45**: Present Perfect vs Past Simple (General vs Specific)
- **Unit 46**: Comparing Places: Adjectives (Noisy, quiet, modern)
- **Unit 47**: Superlatives: The best in the world (The biggest, the most...)
- **Unit 48**: Comparing Lifestyles (City vs Country)
- **Unit 49**: Transport & Travel Experiences (Flight, journey, trip)
- **Unit 50**: Module 5 Review & Assessment

## Module 6: Rules, Advice & Interactions (Units 51-60)
- **Unit 51**: Modals: Have to & Must (Obligation)
- **Unit 52**: Modals: Mustn't & Don't have to (Prohibition/Lack of necessity)
- **Unit 53**: Modals: Should & Shouldn't (Advice & Suggestions)
- **Unit 54**: Modals: Could & May (Permission & Possibility)
- **Unit 55**: Relative Clauses: Who, Which, That (Defining people/things)
- **Unit 56**: Connectors: Although, So, Because (Linking ideas)
- **Unit 57**: Directions & Map Reading (Advanced wayfinding)
- **Unit 58**: Social English: Apologizing & Requesting (Polite forms)
- **Unit 59**: First Conditional: Real Possibilities (If it rains, I'll stay)
- **Unit 60**: Final A2 Review & Assessment
