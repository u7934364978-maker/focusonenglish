In any office, you will find a variety of supplies that are essential for daily tasks. Common items include pens, notebooks, paper clips, and folders. Keeping these items organized helps you work more efficiently and find what you need quickly.

When you need to restock a specific item, you can say "We are running low on..." or "We need more...". For example, "We are running low on printer paper" or "We need more blue pens". This is a simple way to communicate with your colleagues about office needs.

Take a few minutes today to check the supplies in your workspace. Make a list of anything that needs to be replaced. Being proactive about office supplies ensures that you and your team always have the tools you need to succeed.
