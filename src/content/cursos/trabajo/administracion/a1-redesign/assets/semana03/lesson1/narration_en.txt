In a professional office, it is important to know the different job titles and roles. Common titles include Receptionist, Manager, Director, and Assistant. Each person has specific responsibilities that help the office run smoothly.

When you introduce someone else, use the phrase "This is...". For example, you can say "This is Mr. Jones, our Manager" or "This is Sarah, the Receptionist". This is a simple and polite way to help people get to know each other.

Understanding who is who helps you communicate better and know who to ask for help. Take some time today to learn the names and titles of your colleagues. It makes working together much easier!
