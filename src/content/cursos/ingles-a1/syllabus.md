# A1 Premium Course Syllabus (60 Units)

## Module 1: Basics & Personal Info (Units 1-10)
- **Unit 1**: Hello! Introductions (Greetings, My name is...)
- **Unit 2**: Nice to meet you! (Social basics, 'I am' vs 'You are')
- **Unit 3**: Numbers 1-20 (Counting objects, simple ages)
- **Unit 4**: Alphabet & Spelling (Spelling names, basic objects)
- **Unit 5**: Countries & Nationalities (Where are you from?)
- **Unit 6**: Family Members (Mother, Father, basic possessives)
- **Unit 7**: Colors & Describing Things (The red car, big vs small)
- **Unit 8**: Numbers 20-100 (Prices, more ages, telephone numbers)
- **Unit 9**: Jobs & Occupations (I am a teacher, she is a doctor)
- **Unit 10**: Module 1 Review & Assessment

## Module 2: Daily Life & Routines (Units 11-20)
- **Unit 11**: Telling the Time (What time is it?)
- **Unit 12**: Days of the Week (On Monday, On Tuesday)
- **Unit 13**: Daily Routine (I wake up, I go to work)
- **Unit 14**: Present Simple: I, You, We, They (Habits)
- **Unit 15**: Present Simple: He, She, It (The 's' rule)
- **Unit 16**: At the Cafe (Ordering food and drink)
- **Unit 17**: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner (Meal vocabulary)
- **Unit 18**: Likes & Dislikes (I like coffee, I don't like tea)
- **Unit 19**: Months & Dates (When is your birthday?)
- **Unit 20**: Module 2 Review & Assessment

## Module 3: My World (Units 21-30)
- **Unit 21**: Rooms in a House (Kitchen, Bedroom)
- **Unit 22**: Furniture & Household Items (Table, Chair, Bed)
- **Unit 23**: There is / There are (Describing a room)
- **Unit 24**: Prepositions of Place (In, On, Under, Next to)
- **Unit 25**: My Neighborhood (Park, Supermarket, Cinema)
- **Unit 26**: Giving Directions (Go straight, turn left)
- **Unit 27**: Pets & Animals (Dog, Cat, Bird)
- **Unit 28**: Body Parts (Head, Shoulders, Knees)
- **Unit 29**: Clothes (Shirt, Jeans, Shoes)
- **Unit 30**: Module 3 Review & Assessment

## Module 4: Abilities & Interests (Units 31-40)
- **Unit 31**: Can for Ability (I can swim, I can't fly)
- **Unit 32**: Sports & Hobbies (Playing football, playing guitar)
- **Unit 33**: Adverbs of Frequency (Always, Sometimes, Never)
- **Unit 34**: Using 'And', 'But', 'Because' (Basic connectors)
- **Unit 35**: Free Time Activities (Watching TV, reading books)
- **Unit 36**: Musical Instruments (Piano, Violin, Drums)
- **Unit 37**: Talent & Skills (Who can sing?)
- **Unit 38**: Asking for Permission (Can I...?)
- **Unit 39**: Outdoor Activities (Hiking, Camping)
- **Unit 40**: Module 4 Review & Assessment

## Module 5: Places & Travel (Units 41-50)
- **Unit 41**: At the Airport (Check-in, Passport)
- **Unit 42**: Transport (Bus, Train, Plane)
- **Unit 43**: Travel Verbs (Go, Stay, Visit)
- **Unit 44**: The Weather (It's sunny, it's raining)
- **Unit 45**: Seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter)
- **Unit 46**: At the Hotel (Booking a room)
- **Unit 47**: Holidays & Vacation (At the beach)
- **Unit 48**: Sightseeing (Famous places)
- **Unit 49**: Packing a Suitcase (Necessary items)
- **Unit 50**: Module 5 Review & Assessment

## Module 6: Shopping & Food (Units 51-60)
- **Unit 51**: At the Supermarket (Fruit & Vegetables)
- **Unit 52**: Countable & Uncountable (Basic concepts)
- **Unit 53**: Some & Any (Is there any milk?)
- **Unit 54**: Much & Many (How much is it?)
- **Unit 55**: Clothing Store (Sizes and Prices)
- **Unit 56**: At the Restaurant (Reading a menu)
- **Unit 57**: Cooking & Recipes (Basic verbs)
- **Unit 58**: Health & Illness (I have a headache)
- **Unit 59**: Money & Currency (Pounds, Dollars, Euros)
- **Unit 60**: Module 6 Review & Assessment
