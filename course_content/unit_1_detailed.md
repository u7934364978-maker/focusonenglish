# Unit 1: Greetings & Goodbyes (Detailed Exercise Definition)

**Topic**: Basic Greetings, Goodbyes, and the Verb 'To Be' (Am/Is/Are).
**Objective**: Students will recognize and use common greetings and basic identity statements.

---

### Set 1: Recognition & Foundation (1-10)
1. **Visual Matching**: Match the word "Hello" with an image of two people waving at each other.
2. **Visual Matching**: Match "Good morning" with an image of a sunrise and a clock showing 7:00 AM.
3. **Visual Matching**: Match "Good afternoon" with an image of the sun high in the sky and a clock at 2:00 PM.
4. **Visual Matching**: Match "Good night" with an image of a moon and stars.
5. **Audio Recognition**: Hear "Hi" -> Select the word "Hi" from a list: (A) Bye, (B) Hi, (C) No.
6. **Audio Recognition**: Hear "Goodbye" -> Select the corresponding action: (A) Entering, (B) Leaving, (C) Sleeping.
7. **Meaning Check**: Select the formal greeting: (A) Hey!, (B) Hello, (C) Sup.
8. **Categorization**: Drag "Hello" and "Hi" to the 'Greetings' box.
9. **Categorization**: Drag "Bye" and "See you" to the 'Goodbyes' box.
10. **L1 Match**: Match "Hola" with "Hello".

### Set 2: Concept Acquisition (11-20)
11. **Pattern Identification**: Select the correct pronoun for a picture of a man: (A) She, (B) He, (C) It.
12. **Pattern Identification**: Select the correct pronoun for a picture of a chair: (A) He, (B) She, (C) It.
13. **Verb Selection**: "I ___ a teacher." (A) are, (B) is, (C) am.
14. **Verb Selection**: "You ___ my friend." (A) am, (B) is, (C) are.
15. **Verb Selection**: "He ___ happy." (A) is, (B) am, (C) are.
16. **Formal/Informal Sort**: Categorize "Good morning" as (A) Formal, (B) Informal.
17. **Formal/Informal Sort**: Categorize "Hi" as (A) Formal, (B) Informal.
18. **Logic Check**: If someone says "How are you?", a logical answer is: (A) I am fine, (B) I am a doctor.
19. **Meaning Check**: "Nice to meet you" is used when: (A) You leave a party, (B) You meet someone for the first time.
20. **Contraction recognition**: Match "I am" with its contraction: (A) I'm, (B) I's.

### Set 3: Controlled Practice (21-35)
21. **Gap-fill**: "She ___ Maria." (is)
22. **Gap-fill**: "It ___ a book." (is)
23. **Gap-fill**: "They ___ students." (are)
24. **Sentence Scrambling**: Reorder: [am / I / happy / .]
25. **Sentence Scrambling**: Reorder: [is / He / sad / .]
26. **Sentence Scrambling**: Reorder: [are / We / teachers / .]
27. **Transformation**: Rewrite "I am fine" using a contraction. (I'm fine)
28. **Transformation**: Rewrite "She is tall" using a contraction. (She's tall)
29. **Gap-fill**: "Good ___ (morning/night)" (Image: person sleeping).
30. **Gap-fill**: "See ___ later." (you)
31. **Multiple Choice**: "____ you later!" (A) Look, (B) See, (C) Watch.
32. **Gap-fill**: "It ___ (not/no) is a car." -> Correction: "It is not a car."
33. **Sentence Scrambling**: [nice / meet / you / to / .]
34. **Gap-fill**: "How ___ you?" (are)
35. **Gap-fill**: "I am ___ (fine/find)."

### Set 4: Functional Communication (36-45)
36. **Dialogue Completion**: A: "Hello, I am John." B: "Hi John, ___ Maria." (I am / I'm)
37. **Dialogue Completion**: A: "How are you?" B: "Fine, ___." (thanks / thank)
38. **Situational Response**: Someone says "Goodbye". You say: (A) Hi, (B) Bye.
39. **Situational Response**: You enter a shop at 10:00 AM. You say: (A) Good morning, (B) Good night.
40. **Dialogue Match**: Match "What's your name?" with "I'm Carlos."
41. **Dialogue Match**: Match "How are you?" with "I'm great!"
42. **Dialogue Match**: Match "See you tomorrow" with "See you!"
43. **Situational Choice**: You meet a client. Best greeting: (A) Yo!, (B) Hello, how are you?
44. **Logic Check**: If you are leaving, you say: (A) See you, (B) I am.
45. **Dialogue Flow**: Order the conversation: 1. Hello, 2. How are you?, 3. I'm fine, thanks.

### Set 5: Active Production (46-55)
46. **Transcription**: Hear "I am happy" -> Type the sentence exactly.
47. **Transcription**: Hear "She is a student" -> Type the sentence exactly.
48. **Translation**: "Yo soy un estudiante." -> "I am a student."
49. **Translation**: "Él es mi amigo." -> "He is my friend."
50. **Transcription**: Hear "Nice to meet you" -> Type it.
51. **Translation**: "Hola, ¿cómo estás?" -> "Hello, how are you?"
52. **Translation**: "Hasta luego." -> "See you later."
53. **Transcription**: Hear "Good afternoon" -> Type it.
54. **Translation**: "Ella es inteligente." -> "She is intelligent."
55. **Transcription**: Hear "It is a pen" -> Type it.

### Set 6: Synthesis & Mastery (56-60)
56. **Error Correction**: Find the error: "I are happy." -> "I am happy."
57. **Error Correction**: Find the error: "She is my friend?" (Missing question mark or wrong word order? Let's fix form: "Is she my friend?")
58. **Logic Challenge**: Categorize 5 words into 'Greeting', 'Goodbye', or 'Verb To Be'.
59. **Context Mastery**: Complete a mini-paragraph introducing yourself: "Hello, my name ___ John. I ___ a student. I ___ happy."
60. **Fast Recall**: 30 seconds to match 5 greetings to their corresponding times of day.
