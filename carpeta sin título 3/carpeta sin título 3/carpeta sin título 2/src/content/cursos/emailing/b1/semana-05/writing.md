# Writing (B1) · Semana 05

> Objetivo: escribir un recap profesional en 6–10 líneas (claro y neutro).

## 1) Plantilla · Recap (copy/paste)
**Subject:** Recap: [topic]

Hi [Name],

Here’s a quick recap of [meeting/call].

**Key points**
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]

**Action items**
- [Name] will [action] by [day/date]
- I’ll [action] by [day/date]

If anything is missing, please let me know.

Best regards,  
[Your name]

---

## 2) Checklist rápido
- [ ] ¿Subject claro (Recap + tema)?
- [ ] ¿Key points en bullets (no párrafo largo)?
- [ ] ¿Action items con owner + deadline?
- [ ] ¿Cierre con next step o “If anything is missing…”?

---

## 3) Mini-tareas
1) Escribe un recap con 2 key points + 2 action items (owner + deadline).
2) Reescribe un recap largo en formato escaneable (bullets).
