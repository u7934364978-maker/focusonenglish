# Writing (B1) · Semana 03

> Objetivo: proponer horarios y reprogramar con estructura clara + tono neutro.

## 1) Plantilla · Proponer horarios (copy/paste)
**Subject:** Scheduling a quick call

Hi [Name],

Would [day] at [time] work for you?  
If not, I’m also available [option B] or [option C].

Please let me know which option works best.

Best regards,  
[Your name]

---

## 2) Plantilla · Reschedule (copy/paste)
**Subject:** Rescheduling our call

Hi [Name],

Sorry for the change—something came up.  
Would it be possible to reschedule our call?

Could we move it to [day] at [time]?

Thanks in advance.  
Kind regards,  
[Your name]

---

## 3) Checklist rápido
- [ ] ¿Ofreces 2–3 opciones concretas?
- [ ] ¿Incluyes pregunta clara (“Does … work for you?”)?
- [ ] ¿Si reprogramas: disculpa breve + nueva opción?
- [ ] ¿Cierre con next step (calendar invite / meeting link)?

---

## 4) Mini-tareas
1) Escribe un email proponiendo 3 horarios para una call de 15 minutos.
2) Escribe un reschedule con disculpa breve + nueva propuesta.
